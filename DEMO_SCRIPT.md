# Daman demo script (target: 1 minute 50 seconds)

## 0:00–0:12 — The problem

Open on a WhatsApp conversation or a simple title slide.

> “I am buying this calculator through a DM. The seller does not want to hand it over before payment, and I do not want to pay before I inspect it. Daman protects both sides.”

## 0:12–0:32 — Seller creates the deal

- Open Daman.
- Connect the seller wallet.
- Create “Used scientific calculator.”
- Set a small MON price and a short deadline.
- Submit the real Mainnet transaction.
- Copy the generated secure link.

## 0:32–0:52 — Buyer secures payment

- Open the link in a second browser/wallet.
- Point out the public terms, seller, deadline, and exact price.
- Fund the deal.
- Show the UI change to “Payment is locked in Daman.”

## 0:52–1:18 — Physical handoff

- Return to the seller view and display the handoff QR.
- Scan it as the buyer.
- Say: “I inspect the calculator first. Only then do I approve.”
- Confirm the release transaction.
- Show the status change to Completed.

## 1:18–1:35 — Verifiable settlement

- Open the transaction on Monadscan.
- Show the verified DamanEscrow contract and real transfer.

## 1:35–1:50 — Safety case and close

- Briefly show a second expired/refunded deal.
- Close with:

> “The buyer never sends first. The seller never travels for an unfunded promise. Daman: inspect it, scan it, settle it.”

## Recording checklist

- Keep the complete video below 3 minutes.
- Use real contract transactions, not edited success screens.
- Pre-fund both low-value wallets and confirm the correct network.
- Increase browser zoom enough that wallet confirmations and transaction hashes are readable.
- Do not reveal seed phrases, private keys, account emails, or wallet balances unrelated to the demo.
- Publish the video publicly and test the link while signed out.
