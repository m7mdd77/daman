# Security policy

Daman is a hackathon-stage protocol and has not received a third-party audit. Use only amounts you can afford to lose.

## Scope

Security reports may cover:

- Unauthorized escrow withdrawal
- Double settlement or double refund
- Reentrancy
- Buyer or seller authorization bypass
- Incorrect deadline enforcement
- Frontend transaction substitution

Do not test vulnerabilities with other users' funds or against deals you do not own. Reproduce contract findings on a local chain or Monad Testnet.

## Reporting

Until a dedicated security address is published, open a GitHub security advisory rather than a public issue. Include reproduction steps, impact, and affected commit or contract address.

## Product boundary

Daman currently supports in-person handoffs only. It does not arbitrate item quality, shipping, identity, legality, or offchain disputes. Deal titles and terms are public blockchain data.
